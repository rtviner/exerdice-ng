How to Play

  • Enter the number of rounds you would like to play below.
  • Click "Play" or press "Enter" to begin the game.
  • Once the game begins, click on the die or "Roll" or press "Enter" to roll the dice.
  • Each number on the die represents a different exercise.
  • Alternate between the main exercise and partner exercise to complete each round.
  • Warning: The exercise difficulty increases each time you roll a number previously rolled.
