How to Play

  1. Enter the number of rounds you would like to play below.
  2. Click "Play" or press "Enter" to begin the game.
  3. Once the game begins, click on the die or "Roll" or press "Enter" to roll the dice.
  4. Alternate between the main exercise and partner exercise to complete each round.

Warning:

The difficulty of the exercise increases each time you roll a number previously rolled.

Please choose the number of rounds to play.
Please choose between 2 and 8 rounds.
